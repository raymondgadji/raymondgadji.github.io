Our history

Our store is specialised in handmade products by Ivorian producers. Created in 2024, everything here is direct producers without intermediary in our sales circuit. Therefore all our producers are remunerated by setting the prices of their own products themselves.

Our prices

Our prices are the lowest possible enabling us to make profits while feeding correctly our handmade Ivorian producers. Our policy of lower prices is suitable to both our producers and our customers .
